Who We Are

Rise African Child Media is a creative digital platform that aims to tell stories in a the TV and film industry, stories that can change the enviroment, we want to educate and empower young upcoming entrepreneurs and film makers, our goal is to create employment and help these young creative's archives and persue their dreams.
